(10 points)
Let $u$ be a smooth function satisfying
\begin{align*}
  -\Delta u + u = f(x) & |u| \leq 1 & x \in \reals^n
\end{align*}
where
\begin{align*}
  |f(x)| \leq C e^{-|x|}
\end{align*}
Deduce from the maximum principle that $u$ actually decays
\begin{align*}
  |u(x)| \leq C e^{-\frac{1}{2} |x|}
\end{align*}
